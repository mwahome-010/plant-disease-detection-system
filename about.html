About Farmer's Center

Farmer's Center is a plant disease detection system, designed to help farmers identify and manage plant diseases.

Our AI-powered tool analyzes images of plants to detect diseases and provide recommendations for treatment. Furthermore, we offer a detailed database of agricultural knowledge and a community forum for farmers to connect, aid and share experiences.

Our Mission

To provide farmers with accessible, accurate, and actionable agricultural knowledge through innovative technology and community support.

What We Offer

Modern tools and resources to help you succeed in modern farming
